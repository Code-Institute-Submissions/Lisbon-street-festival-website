The Festival

Welcome to the international Lisbon Street Art Festival! The event is organized by Shape our City in partnership with the Lisbon City council.

At its second edition, the festival will welcome over 90 artist from Portugal and abroad.

Check out the program of the festival and the calendar of events from August the 15th to August the 20th!
